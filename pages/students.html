Students of Eskisehir Technical University

Eskisehir Technical University is one of the leading technical universities in Turkey. The university has students enrolled in various departments. Most students live on or near the campus premises in Eskisehir.

Student Activities

Students of Eskisehir Technical University engage in various social and cultural activities alongside their academic studies. They participate in student clubs and organizations, sports events, cultural festivals, and social service projects.

Student Life

Eskisehir is a student-friendly city and hosts a large number of students. The city offers a wide range of social and cultural opportunities for students, including cafes, restaurants, cinemas, theaters, and other entertainment venues.
